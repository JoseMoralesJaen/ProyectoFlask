Help


What is this web for ?
  • This web serves as an application for a CBR system. The purpose of this work is to recommend cocktails according to a set of ingredients.
Where can I find the cocktails ?
  • There is a catalog with more than 100 cocktail recipes in "cocktail" tab.
What happens if the cocktail that I want is not in the catalog?
  • This is not a problem, in "Specially for you" you can choose the ingredients that you want for your cocktail and we will provide you a recipe.
How many ingredients can I choose?
  • You can choose as many you want with a minimum of 2 ingredients.
After receiving a personalized recipe I noticed that I am allergic to some ingredients, what can I do?
  • Just email us to cocktailmasters@barkeeper.com and we will provide you a new recipe according to your needs.
Can I trust the recommended recipe ?
  • Most of the time we offer feasible recipes, however it may happen that the recipe does not follow what you are looking for. For that reason we ask for your opinion. In that way you can help us to keep improving!
